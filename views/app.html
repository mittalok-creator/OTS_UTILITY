About this calculator

Search any borrower by Account No., Customer ID, Mobile, Aadhar or PAN. Every field is pulled from the NPA master and OLD OTS registers using the same lookup logic as the original Excel sheet — only the OTS Amount is yours to enter.

This is a shared internal tool. Figures are calculated on the server from the data your bank has uploaded; nothing is sent to any third party.

Update Data

Upload a new workbook (.xlsx) containing an NPA sheet (and optionally an OLD OTS sheet), or upload your daily e-AB NPA AC WISE CSV export straight from the CBS — it's parsed automatically. This replaces the data used by the calculator for everyone signed in.
